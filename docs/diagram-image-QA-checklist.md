# RallyReady — Diagram Image QA Checklist

Run **every** AI-generated diagram against this list (zoom in to read text and
count figures) before accepting it. If any item fails, tell the model exactly
what's wrong and regenerate until it passes.

## A. Court & viewpoint
1. Correct viewpoint — **top-down** for court diagrams, clean **side-on** for Tips. No 3D / isometric tilt.
2. Net is a **dashed line straight across the middle** (horizontal) with a small **post dot at each end**.
3. Attack (3-meter) line on each side where required.
4. **Near side at the bottom**, far side at the top.
5. Court = soft rounded **grey** rectangle on an **off-white** background.

## B. Players (the figures)
6. Real friendly **female** figures — **not discs, not stick figures, not male**.
7. **Correct count** on each side (count them: 3v3, 2 servers, 6 base, etc.).
8. **Correct colors** — coral = your team, teal = other team, grey = waiting/neutral. Never mixed up.
9. **Correct FACING — players face the net / ball / action.**
   - Top-down: the **near (bottom)** team is seen **from behind** (backs to viewer, facing **up** toward the net); the **far (top)** team is seen **from the front** (facing **down** toward the net / viewer). The two sides **face each other** across the net.
   - Nobody facing off the court or away from the play.
10. Players at realistic court positions, evenly spaced (not bunched, not a random row).
11. No extra / duplicate / merged figures; no missing or extra limbs; no distorted anatomy.

## C. Ball, arrows & motion
12. Ball = simple **white sphere with two curved seam lines** (wherever a ball appears).
13. Arrows go the **right direction** and connect the **right points** (e.g., serve arcs **over the net** into the target zone).
14. **Correct number** of arrows; smooth/tapered; not tangled or doubled.
15. Side-view Tips: ball flight = **tapered dotted trail** with the ball near the **end** of the path.

## D. Labels & legend
16. **All text spelled correctly** — no gibberish. Zoom in and actually read it.
17. Correct labels/letters (S, P, St, H, zone numbers, role abbreviations) on the **right** element.
18. Legend present where the template needs one, **color-coded** correctly.
19. Right numbers (zones 1 & 5; rotation order 1 → 6 → 5 → 4 → 3 → 2; etc.).

## E. Style & consistency
20. **One consistent player art style across the whole set** (we use the realistic
    illustrated female players — near players shown from behind — to match the
    existing 10 images). Don't mix cute front-view characters with realistic back-view.
21. Clean even line weights, soft shadows, high contrast, generous negative space, off-white/transparent background.
22. No photorealism, no busy background, no watermark/signature/logo.
